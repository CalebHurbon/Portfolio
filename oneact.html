About this Show

As my senior project for college, I had to create a light design for a student devised production. The most challenging part was learning how much time each part of the design process takes.
My favorite part about this show was the 1920's scene called for in the script. Using a small moving light I shot it full power on the back wall creating what looked like a moon against a black (night) wall! The best part is that the light was reflecting off the wall onto the stage below; the way actual moonlight would!
